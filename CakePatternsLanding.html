Cake Patterns

2023
Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop
Academic

The objective of this project was to choose a theme and interpret it 100 ways. This is the last half of the project, problems 41 to 100. The theme I chose was "cake" and for the last half of the project, I decided to do patterns because I like the repetitiveness of patterns and the shift in mood that happens when you make a small variation such as color. Problems 41 to 50: typography-centric, keeping a constant color palette while translating cake in different languages. Problems 51 to 80: while the form of cake changes every set of 10-- cupcake, cheesecake, square cake--the format is the same with changing the background and cake color. Problems 81 - 100: the cake color changes while the background color remains the same.
