Catalog Spread Ad

2023
Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop
Academic

This was part of a collaborative project in which we were to design a useless gadget and create two advertisements. My partner and I designed shoes that played music to hide noisy squeaky shoes, resulting in more noise rather than less. Inspired by mail-in catalogs, my ad mimics a catalog spread showcasing all the designed shoes. The idea was to have our ads operate as two halves of a whole. The QR code is linked to my partner's ad, which advertises the shoes' selling features. His ad would be the one to hook viewers into buying the shoes and my ad would be the place they would pick a specific shoe and order from. Given the shoes are named SonicSoles--from Walkin' Sunshine Co.--the sneakers are themed around Sonic characters which can be seen in their color palette and names.
