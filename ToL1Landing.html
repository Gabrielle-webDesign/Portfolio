Theater of Light #1

2021
Acrylics on illustration board (16" x 14")
Academic

This project was a light study from a shadow box of our own creation. The shadows casted and painted were from a subtractive light source, or light from the foreground. I find that the scene depicted almost mimics a still frame from a black and white cartoon.
