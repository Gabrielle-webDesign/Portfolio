Book Cover: The Lost Boys

2024
Canva (5.5" x 8.5")
Personal

A book cover redesign of Craig Shaw Gardner’s The Lost Boys, a novelization of Joel Schumacher's film The Lost Boys (1987), an iconic movie for horror/vampire genre and overall 80s movies. With the idea that I would be marketing the book to fans of the movie and inspired by minimalist book covers, the main centerpiece is the silhouette of the Santa Cruz boardwalk in California, the filming site of the movie. In traditional marketing of the film red is the predominant color with black and white being secondary. I decided to do an inverted color scheme–black being dominant with red as secondary–to keep it more aligned to the book’s original design. The title, however, is kept the same across both mediums and I followed suit, using a font that closely resembles the original and using the same layout.
