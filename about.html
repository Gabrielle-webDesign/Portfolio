About Me

Hello! I’m Gabrielle Savage, though you might know me as Sister Dora through my avatar. I’m a Digital Arts major at La Salle University in Philadelphia, PA, with a passion for storytelling through visuals. My work is inspired by animation, character design, and book arts—from cover design to bookbinding. While I draw from diverse digital influences, my heart lies in traditional art, where I specialize in painting and sketching to bring unique characters and stories to life.
