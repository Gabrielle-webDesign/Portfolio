Self-Portrait

2023
Adobe Photoshop (11" x 17")
Academic

Inspired by silhouette portraits, I subverted the traditional requirement for a self-portrait by omitting my face from the piece and having the silhouette of the original photo be the “face”. Using my high school photo and baby photos, the latter are multicolored outlines to resemble a crayon drawing and represent a childlike innocence while the former is a filled black void to provide contrast for the colored outlines. The white glasses are a complement and contrasting feature for the black silhouette, showing that I didn’t always wear glasses. The pea pod patterned background adds texture to the plain blue backdrop and calls back to an old childhood nickname. The parental advisory sticker was added to convey that there’s more to the story and the comical fact that any image immediately becomes an album cover when you slap a parental advisory label on it, which album covers featuring the artist’s baby photos were my secondary inspiration.
